Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for startups? +

We specialise in company incorporation, Startup India recognition, ESOP advisory, GST registration, annual compliance, and funding due diligence. We've helped 50+ Mumbai startups from pre-incorporation to Series A.

What's included in the Virtual CFO plan? +

At ₹10,000/month: monthly MIS reports, cash flow projections, P&L analysis, board-ready presentations, and investor reporting. Ideal for Series A-stage companies that need CFO-level insight without a full-time hire.

How long does company registration take? +

7–10 working days. Includes DSC, DIN, name reservation, MCA SPICe+ filing, and post-incorporation PAN, TAN, GST registration. All-inclusive packages from ₹8,000.

Do you handle FEMA & foreign investment compliance? +

Yes — FC-GPR, FC-TRS filings, ODI reporting, transfer pricing documentation, and foreign remittance advisory for companies with international investors or cross-border operations.

Can you help recover GST refunds? +

Absolutely. We file refund claims for exporters, inverted duty structure, excess ITC, and cancelled registrations. We track status and liaise with GST officers — most refunds processed within 60 days.
