🛠️ What We Do

Everything your startup's
finances need. Nothing it doesn't.

We built our service stack specifically for startups — from day-zero incorporation to Series A readiness.

🏢

Startup Incorporation

Pvt Ltd, LLP, or One Person Company — we handle the full setup. Name approval, MOA/AOA drafting, PAN, TAN, GST registration. You get a company ready to operate in under 10 days.

💰

Fundraising Tax Advisory

Raising from angels or VCs? We structure your round to be tax-efficient. Section 56(2)(viib) valuations, SAFE/CCD conversions, and DPIIT exemptions handled end to end.

📋

ESOP Structuring

Attract top talent without burning cash. We design ESOP pools, draft schemes compliant with Companies Act, and handle the tax implications for both you and your team.

📊

MIS & Financial Reports

Investor-ready MIS dashboards delivered monthly. P&L, cash flow, burn rate, runway — all the numbers your board actually wants to see, in a format that makes sense.

🧠

Virtual CFO

A real finance brain on your team — without the ₹40L salary. Strategic financial planning, cash flow management, fundraise prep, and board deck support. Part-time CFO, full-time impact.

🔒

Compliance & Tax Filing

GST returns, TDS, income tax, ROC filings, Startup India registration — all on autopilot. We track every deadline so you never pay a penalty or miss a filing.
