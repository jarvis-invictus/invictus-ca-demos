Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Virtual CFO service? +

At Rs 8,000/month: monthly MIS reports, cash flow analysis, P&L review, board presentation prep, and fundraise advisory. Strategic financial guidance without a full-time CFO hire.

What is ROC compliance and why do I need it? +

Every private limited company must file annual returns (MGT-7), financial statements (AOC-4), director KYC, and board minutes with the ROC. Penalties for non-compliance range from Rs 100/day to Rs 1 lakh+. We handle everything from Rs 3,500/year.

Can you handle GST department notices? +

Yes. We handle scrutiny, demand, and mismatch notices. Our team drafts responses, attends hearings, and resolves disputes to protect you from penalties.

What makes Verdant Finance different? +

Advisory-first approach -- we proactively identify tax savings and flag compliance gaps before they become problems. Ethical, transparent pricing. Long-term financial health over shortcuts.

What does statutory audit cost? +

Statutory audit starts at Rs 18,000 depending on turnover and complexity. Required for all companies with turnover above Rs 1 crore. We deliver detailed audit reports with actionable findings.
