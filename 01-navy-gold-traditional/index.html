Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does GST filing cost? +

GST filing plans start at ₹1,500/month for businesses with up to 50 transactions. Packages include GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and annual return filing with dedicated CA support. We offer quarterly and annual plans for small businesses.

Can you help with company registration in Pune? +

Yes. We handle Private Limited, LLP, OPC, and Partnership registrations. The process takes 7–10 working days and includes MCA filing, PAN, TAN, and GST registration. Free post-registration compliance advisory included.

Do you offer online CA services? +

Yes. All services including ITR filing, GST compliance, and financial advisory are available remotely via WhatsApp, email, and video call. No need to visit the office for most services — we've served clients across India since 2018.

What is the ITR filing deadline and penalty? +

The standard ITR deadline is July 31st for individuals and October 31st for businesses requiring audit. Missing it incurs a ₹5,000 penalty (₹1,000 for income below ₹5 lakh). We send proactive reminders to all clients well before deadlines.

How quickly can I get my income tax refund? +

Tax refunds through our e-filing service typically arrive within 20–45 days of filing. We track your refund status and follow up on delays with the Income Tax Department on your behalf.
