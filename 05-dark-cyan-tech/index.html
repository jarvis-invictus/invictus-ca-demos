COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Angel Tax and how can a startup avoid it? +

Angel Tax (Sec 56(2)(viib)) applies when a startup raises funding above fair market value from resident investors. DPIIT recognition provides full exemption. We handle DPIIT filing and Angel Tax compliance to protect your startup.

How do IT freelancers handle GST in India? +

Freelancers above Rs 20L/year need GST registration. Exports to foreign clients are zero-rated (0% GST) via LUT. We handle GST, ITR-3/4 filing, and LRS forex reporting for Pune's tech freelancers.

What are Section 10A/10AA tax exemptions for IT companies? +

100% income tax exemption on software export profits for 5 years (SEZ units), then 50% for 5 more. We assess eligibility and structure your ops to maximise these exemptions legally.

How does Cipher and Co handle ESOP taxation? +

ESOP tax hits at exercise (perquisite) and sale (capital gains). DPIIT startups can defer exercise tax up to 5 years. We design structures, calculate liability, handle Form 16 reporting, and advise on optimal exercise timing.

Do you serve SaaS companies with international clients? +

Yes -- ARR/MRR reporting, export GST exemption (LUT/IGST refund), transfer pricing, FEMA compliance, and Virtual CFO with SaaS metrics (churn, CAC, LTV) on monthly dashboards.
