Our Story

Three Generations,
One Promise

It all started with Late CA Vishwanath Kulkarni in 1974 — a small desk, a typewriter, and a passion for helping people understand their taxes. He believed that every family deserves honest financial guidance, not just the wealthy ones.

His son CA Sunil Kulkarni grew the practice through the 90s and 2000s, guiding hundreds of Pune families through economic reforms, new tax laws, and the arrival of IT returns. Many of his early clients are still with us today.

Today, CA Sachin Kulkarni (third generation) leads the firm with the same values — patience, clarity, and genuine care. We've added technology and online filing, but the personal touch remains the same.

Common Questions

Questions People
Always Ask Us

No question is silly — we've heard them all and we love explaining!

Haan, bilkul! Even if your income is below the taxable limit, filing ITR has many benefits — you can claim TDS refund, it helps in getting loans, visa applications, and it's a good financial habit. We file basic ITR starting at just ₹999. Don't worry, we'll check everything for you!
If your annual turnover is below ₹40 lakh (goods) or ₹20 lakh (services), GST registration is optional. But sometimes voluntary registration helps — especially for online selling or if your customers need GST bills. Come have chai with us, we'll explain what's best for your specific business!
Poora kaam online ho sakta hai! Bas WhatsApp pe apne documents bhejo — Form 16, Aadhaar, PAN, bank statement — aur hum sab handle kar lenge. But if you want to sit and discuss, hamara office Tilak Road pe hai. Chai pakka milegi! 🍵
We always tell you the fee upfront — no hidden charges, guaranteed. Personal ITR starts at ₹999, business ITR from ₹2,999, and GST monthly filing from ₹1,500. Complex cases are discussed and quoted before starting. Hamara motto hai — transparent fees, trusted service!
Property sale par capital gains tax lagta hai — how much depends on when you bought it, for how much, and what improvements you made. There are also ways to save tax under Section 54 (buying another property) or 54EC (bonds). Bring your purchase agreement and we'll calculate exact tax + legal ways to save. Many people overpay because they don't know about indexation benefit!
Ghabrao mat! Most notices are routine — just verification or minor mismatches. Don't ignore it and don't panic. WhatsApp us the notice photo immediately — we'll read it, explain what it means, and respond on your behalf. In 50 years, we've handled thousands of notices successfully. You're in safe hands! 💪
